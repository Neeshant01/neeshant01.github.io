Tips and Resources

Start with exposure first

Fix brightness and contrast before changing too many colors. Good light usually improves everything else.

Use one clear mood

Warm, cinematic, or monochrome edits feel stronger when you commit to one direction instead of mixing everything.

Export for the web

Compressed JPEG or modern formats help websites load faster while still keeping enough detail for profile photos.
