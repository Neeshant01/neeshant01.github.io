How to Play

Slip in, steal the signal, then disappear before the lock closes.

  • Move with `WASD` or arrow keys and stay outside guard paths and camera beams.
  • Press `E` to hack a nearby node and cut cameras for a short window.
  • Press `Q` to fire a distraction ping and pull patrols off line.
  • Steal the target, then escape through the exit with the lowest alert you can manage.
