Arena Notes

Each level tracks its own board. Faster clears and cleaner routes score higher.

How to Play

Run the maze, collect nodes, and stay ahead of the seeker.

  • Move with `WASD` or arrow keys across the grid.
  • Pick BFS, DFS, or A* before the round. The seeker uses that search style against you.
  • Collect every data node, then reach the exit portal.
  • Fast clean clears unlock the next level and save to that level board.
