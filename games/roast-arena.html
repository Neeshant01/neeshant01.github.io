How to Play

Dodge the heat, charge the mic, then fire the comeback.

  • Move with `A` `D` or arrow keys. Hold `S` or `Down` to duck low shots.
  • Survive each wave to charge the mic meter.
  • Use `1`, `2`, or `3` to launch a comeback style when the mic is hot.
  • Crowd energy decides the round. Beat bigger opponents to unlock the next tier.
